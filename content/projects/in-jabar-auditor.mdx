# In-Jabar, Auditor Platform

## Problem

Inspektorat Daerah Provinsi Jawa Barat IRBAN IV ran its audit cycles on a
paper- and email-driven workflow. They needed a digital platform with a
document viewer, structured audit cycle data, and email notifications, and a
team that could ship it in one quarter.

## What I built

I led a 4-person team as **Tech Lead** (1 frontend, 1 backend, 1 mobile, 1 QA):

- Designed full architecture and selected the stack
  (Next.js + MUI on the frontend, Express + MongoDB on the backend).
- Modeled the audit workflow domain so a single source of truth could serve
  web, mobile, and reporting.
- Defined the **REST + JWT contract** that the web frontend and mobile client
  share.
- Owned client communication with Pemprov Jawa Barat across the engagement.

Implementation of feature work was done by the team; my role was design,
review, and unblock.

## Outcomes

- Live in the Pemprov Jabar internal environment since **September 2024**.
- 3 months from kickoff to go-live (Jul–Sep 2024).

## Tech highlights

- **node-forge** for cryptographic document signing, audit-trail integrity
  matters for a public-sector use case.
- **Winston** structured logging for compliance-grade audit logs.
- **@mui/x-charts** drives the auditor analytics dashboard.
- **Shared REST + JWT contract** lets the web and mobile clients consume the
  same API without divergence.
- **pm2** for zero-downtime restarts in production.
