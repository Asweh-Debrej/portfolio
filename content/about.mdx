## Why this site looks like a desktop

Because static portfolios are everywhere. I wanted something **memorable** that
also doubles as a small engineering portfolio piece in its own right, a custom
window manager, MDX content pipeline, and accessible keyboard-first UX, all
shipped as a static export with a tiny First Load JS budget.

> Tip: try right-clicking the desktop, or pressing `Shift + Esc` for the start
> menu.
